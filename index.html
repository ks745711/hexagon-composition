Hexagon Composition

Create a moving composition of hexagons to feast your eyes on. Try out super speed, but also slow-mo. Download a picture at your preferred hexagon density to use as a background, or just to remember this experience by.

Parameters

  • Size: The size of each individual hexagon.
  • Horizontal speed: The speed of all hexagons currently traveling on path in a horizontal direction.
  • Vertical speed: The speed of all hexagons currently traveling on path in a vertical direction
  • Extra hexes: The amount of individual hexagons on the screen.
